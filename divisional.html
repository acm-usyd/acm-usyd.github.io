International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC) 2019 South Pacific Region - Central Division: Sydney Site

Welcome to the Sydney site of the South Pacific Region Divisional Contest, which is part of the International Collegiate Programming Contest (ICPC). Results from the Divisional Contest will be used to determine which teams go on to compete in the Regional Final Contest, which will take place at UNSW Sydney in November 2019.

Teams of 3 students work together to solve about 12 problems within a 5 hour period. Each team only has access to one computer. Programs may be written in Java, C, C++, or Python. The contest involves problem solving, programming, and lots of teamwork.

The Sydney site is hosted by the School of Computer Science at the University of Sydney.

Time and Venue

  • Host: School of Computer Science, Faculty of Engineering, The University of Sydney
